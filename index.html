Summary

Graduated with honors from Yıldız Technical University, I am an architect with a passion for integrating design and technology. My architectural education has equipped me with a strong foundation in spatial design, aesthetics, and problem-solving. However, my curiosity has led me to explore the world of programming and web design, bridging the gap between architecture and digital technology.

Following my graduation, I proactively formed a team and delved into various urban and architectural design competitions. Our collaborative efforts resulted in notable achievements such as a 3rd Honorable Mention in the Büyükada Phaeton Square Urban Design Competition and a Jury Special Award in the Topkapı Kaleiçi Square Urban Design Competition.

In 2022, I expanded my academic horizons by enrolling in the Urban Design master's program at Istanbul Technical University. Here, I have been able to merge my architectural background with digital technology, experimenting with computational design tools and exploring the potential of machine learning in urban planning.

During my master's program, our team was recognized as finalists in the TerraViva "Tactical Urbanism Now! 2023" Competition. We are currently working on transforming our proposed project for earthquake-prone areas in Turkey into a social initiative.

With a growing interest and proficiency in programming and web design, I am excited to announce my aspiration to continue my career as a front-end developer. I am enthusiastic about leveraging my combined expertise in architecture and digital technology to create intuitive and engaging user experiences. As I continue my studies at Istanbul Technical University, I am committed to honing my front-end development skills and contributing to innovative projects that blend design and technology.
